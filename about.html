FAQ

Answers before the build starts.

What makes these upgrades OEM?

The process is centered on genuine factory components and factory style conversion pathways, so the parts, fitment, and finished result stay aligned with how the platform was designed to work.

Are the parts genuine factory parts?

Yes. Thewconverts focuses on genuine OEM components selected for the specific build, along with the supporting trim, hardware, and related pieces needed to complete the upgrade properly.

Can a single cab or base model truck be upgraded to a higher trim look and feature set?

Yes. That is one of the main reasons the business exists. The process is built to help owners add premium styling and functionality that the manufacturer often never offered on those models.

How is fitment verified before a build starts?

Fitment is confirmed by reviewing the truck year, trim, and factory options, then matching the correct OEM parts, conversion kit, harness, and supporting hardware to that exact setup.

When is a harness solution included?

If the upgrade involves wiring, a plug and play harness solution is included when needed so the installation stays clean and the final result keeps a factory style appearance.

Can I install the kit myself, or can the team handle the work?

Either route can work. Owners can install the kit themselves, or the team can handle the installation. The parts plan is organized to make the process more straightforward and reduce downtime.

How do you protect factory style fit and function?

The build path starts with OEM parts, compatibility review, and platform specific knowledge. That approach helps preserve clean fitment, proper operation, and a finish that feels like it belongs on the truck.
